What You'll Configure

  • Maximum Panel Depth: How deep treatment panels can be
  • Allowed Surfaces: Which walls/ceilings can receive treatment
  • Forbidden Areas: Windows, doors, and other unavailable spaces
  • Symmetry Rules: Left-right treatment symmetry requirements
  • Budget Class: Basic, standard, or premium treatment options
  • Treatment Mode: Diffusion-focused, absorption-focused, or balanced

Space-Specific Considerations

🎛️ Studio / Control Room
  • Enforce left-right symmetry
  • Avoid near-field diffusion
  • Control first reflections
  • Precision treatment placement
🎧 Hi-Fi Listening Room
  • Balance diffusion and absorption
  • Focus on rear wall treatment
  • Maintain spaciousness
  • Flexible placement options
🏛️ Public Space
  • Audience coverage priority
  • Speech intelligibility focus
  • Large area treatment
  • Distributed approach
