Constraint Explanations

Max Panel Depth: Limits the maximum thickness of treatment panels. Deeper panels affect low frequencies more but take up more space.
Symmetry Required: When enabled, treatment recommendations will be symmetrical left-to-right, typical for critical listening rooms.
Treatment Mode: Balanced: mix of diffusion and absorption. Diffusion First: prioritize diffusers. Absorption First: prioritize absorbers.
Budget Class: Influences the complexity and number of treatment recommendations. Low: minimal treatment. High: comprehensive treatment.
