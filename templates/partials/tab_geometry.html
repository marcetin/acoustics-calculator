What You'll Configure

  • Room Type: Rectangular (shoebox) or custom polyhedral geometry
  • Dimensions: Room length, width, and height
  • Wall Setup: Number and arrangement of walls
  • Ceiling Height: Vertical space configuration
  • Room Volume: Automatically calculated from dimensions
ℹ️
Geometry is Required

Room geometry must be configured before you can set up surfaces, sources, receivers, or run acoustic analysis.
